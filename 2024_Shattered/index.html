Shattered

Painting

View project

"Shattered" reflects the fragmentation of society due to the widespread influence of social media, particularly platforms like TikTok. The painting portrays the disintegration of human connection and communication as we increasingly engage through digital lenses. This piece was sold at the Rochester Contemporary Art Center as part of their fundraiser, contributing both to the arts and to an important conversation about the impact of social media on society.

Medium: Oil on Canvas (6”x6”)

The original painting was created for a student ceiling-tile installation at Middleton High School. Following changes to the display program, the artwork was removed and eventually lost. This version was recreated to preserve both the original image and the ideas it explored.
