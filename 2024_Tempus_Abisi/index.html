Tempus Abissi

Painting

View project

Tempus Abissi visually explores the infinite and irreversible nature of time. At its center is a spiraling staircase made of sand, representing time's relentless forward motion and impermanence. As the sand erodes and slips away, it symbolizes the fleeting nature of each moment, once lost, it cannot be reclaimed. The piece serves as a metaphor for the human experience of time, emphasizing both its continuous progression and the transient quality of life.

Medium: Oil on Canvas (8”x10”)
