Concept Footwear Design

A Study in Form, Material, and Color

View project

This project explores digital product visualization through the design of a futuristic athletic shoe. Using Adobe Photoshop, I developed a cohesive visual language by combining color gradients, material textures, lighting, and graphic overlays to emphasize movement, speed, and performance. The objective was to create a believable product concept while exploring how color, surface finish, and composition influence the perception of modern footwear.

The visual identity draws inspiration from futuristic performance footwear. Flowing contours, layered gradients, and luminous accents communicate motion, while the contrast between matte and glossy surfaces creates depth and highlights the sculptural qualities of the shoe. The color palette reinforces a sense of innovation and energy without overwhelming the overall form.
