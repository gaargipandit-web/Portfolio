Still Life

Painting

View project

This piece represents one of my earliest experiences working with oil paint. The composition, arranged by my teacher, challenged me to explore the texture and depth that oil paint can provide. This still life allowed me to practice blending colors, working with a slow-drying medium, and capturing the fine details of the objects.

Medium: Oil on Canvas (8”x10”)
