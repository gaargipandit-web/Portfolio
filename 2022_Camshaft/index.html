Camshaft Adventure

Designing play through mechanical motion and simple machines.

View project brief Photo Gallery

Project Brief

One of my favorite aspects of design is creating objects that teach through interaction. This project explores how a simple mechanical camshaft can transform rotational motion into animated storytelling. I designed every component, from the individual cam profiles and wooden enclosure to the complete mechanical assembly, using Fusion 360 before fabricating a working prototype.

Each cam has a unique profile that controls how characters move as the shaft rotates. By swapping different cams onto the axle, the toy can create entirely different motions and stories. Along the way, I also produced detailed engineering drawings, assembly documentation, and exploded views to communicate how every component fits together.

This project strengthened my understanding of CAD modeling, mechanism design, fabrication planning, and the relationship between digital models and physical prototypes.
