Promotional Design

Café Brand Identity & Promotional Design

View project

Timescape is a themed café branding project centered on the idea that coffee has the power to slow life down and create moments worth savoring. Inspired by concepts of time, infinity, and playful science-fiction references, the project combines a whimsical visual identity with a warm vintage aesthetic. The branding revolves around an ouroboros-inspired logo, symbolizing an endless cycle of time, paired with the tagline “Where Time Stops.” Every promotional element was designed to reinforce the café’s immersive atmosphere, transforming an ordinary coffee shop into a memorable storytelling experience. This project received an Honorable Mention in the 2025 Scholastic Art & Writing Awards (Design category).

The design process focused on creating a cohesive identity across multiple forms of customer interaction. A parchment-inspired color palette, hand-crafted typography, and subtle distressed textures evoke the feeling of an old journal while maintaining a contemporary café aesthetic. The menu extends the narrative through imaginative, time-themed food and drink names, adding personality and encouraging customer engagement. Branded employee apparel, business cards, and logo variations ensure visual consistency across every touchpoint. Key features of the project include cohesive brand identity, immersive storytelling through graphic design, consistent visual language across print and merchandise, thematic menu and typography, custom logo and iconography, vintage-inspired color palette, customer-centered promotional design, and professional presentation suitable for real-world branding applications.

The accompanying images document the complete branding system. The primary logo establishes the café’s visual identity using the ouroboros motif and coffee cup illustration. Employee shirt designs extend the brand through coordinated front and back graphics, while the business card applies the same visual language to a professional networking format. The custom menu demonstrates how typography, layout, and playful descriptions combine to create an engaging customer experience. Together, these materials illustrate the development of a unified promotional package—from logo creation and print design to wearable branding and customer-facing marketing collateral—demonstrating an integrated approach to visual communication and brand development.
