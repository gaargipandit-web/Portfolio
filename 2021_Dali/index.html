The Acquisition of Power vs. Purity

Painting

View project

The art examines the tension between power and purity through my interpretation of Ovid's Metamorphoses. Inspired by the myth of Apollo and Daphne, the artwork features Daphne transforming into a tree, symbolizing purity and incorruptibility. Surrounding her are powerful technologies, all derived from the sun, a representation of Apollo. I employed colored pencils for the majority of the piece, using graphite to depict the technologies emerging beyond fire. Juxtaposition is a key element, such as placing the sun on the ground near the tree, with collaging techniques used to make the manifestations of power stand out.

 Exhibited at: The Dali Museum

Medium: Colored Pencil on Paper (8”x10”)
