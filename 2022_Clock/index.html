Personalized Medical-Themed Clock

A Personalized Gift Combining CAD, Electronics & Digital Fabrication

View project

This custom desk clock was designed and fabricated as a personalized sixteenth birthday gift for a close friend aspiring to become a medical doctor. The design combines meaningful symbolism with practical functionality, incorporating medical-inspired elements into the enclosure while providing an everyday digital clock. The project demonstrates how engineering and design can be used together to create objects that are both useful and deeply personal.

The enclosure was modeled in Fusion 360 with careful attention to form, proportions, and manufacturability before being fabricated on an Ender-3 3D printer using PLA filament. An Arduino-based electronic system drives the digital display and integrates the clock’s controls within the custom enclosure. By combining CAD modeling, embedded electronics, rapid prototyping, and digital fabrication, the project provided experience across the complete product development workflow—from concept and design through fabrication, assembly, testing, and final presentation.

The accompanying images document each stage of the build process. They include the completed clock displaying its illuminated LED interface, the custom enclosure featuring personalized birthday details, assembly and internal electronics, and the soldering process used to build the circuitry. Together these photographs illustrate the transition from digital design to physical prototype, highlighting the integration of mechanical design, electronics, and additive manufacturing into a fully functional, one-of-a-kind gift.
