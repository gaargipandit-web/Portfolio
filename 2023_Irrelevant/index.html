Irrelevant

Painting

View project

"Irrelevant" addresses the absurdity of racism by drawing inspiration from the Star Trek: The Original Series episode "Let That Be Your Last Battlefield." In the episode, two alien factions are at odds over a seemingly trivial difference—one group has black on the left side of their face and the other on the right. I translated this idea into my artwork by placing two faces side by side, divided only by a bolt of lightning. This bolt symbolizes the violence between the groups, but in its light, the distinction between them becomes indistinguishable, emphasizing the irrelevance of racial differences.

Medium: Acrylic on Construction Paper (8.5”x11”)
