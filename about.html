About This Website

Welcome to my portfolio.

This website was designed and built by me as an extension of my interest in design, art, geometry, and visual storytelling. The layout, structure, visual themes, and project presentations were developed by hand, with some technical assistance for clean up and consistency from ChatGPT.

Unless otherwise noted, all artwork, drawings, renderings, photographs, design projects, and written content displayed on this website are my original work. These materials may not be reproduced, redistributed, modified, or used in any form without my prior permission.

The fractal background imagery used throughout the site was generated using my own Python programs and mathematical visualization techniques. The website itself is intended not only as a collection of projects, but also as a reflection of my interest in the connection between design, mathematics, and creative exploration.

Thank you for visiting and taking the time to explore my work.
