Still Life (Water Color)

Painting

View project

This piece is a progression study of a still life setup featuring a flower in a glass jar and a purple mug. The sequence demonstrates my process from initial sketch to the final completed painting, showcasing my approach to layering, detailing, and bringing realism to the composition.

Working in watercolor, I gradually built up the painting, starting with light washes and carefully adding depth and texture in each stage. The reflections in the glass jar, the delicate petals of the flower, and the subtle shadows on the fabric backdrop are meticulously developed to capture the essence of the objects. This project highlights my patience and technical growth in handling watercolor to create lifelike effects.

Medium: Watercolor on Paper (8" x 8")
