Project Brief

A layered night lamp with negative space expressed through geometric hierarchy. The design uses repeated triangular forms to create depth, shadow, and a soft ambient glow. Our task was to create a layered night light prototype using styrene sheets for layers, chipboard for the base, with a puck light for the light. It is both extremely functional and sculptural!

Durable

A layered structure for everyday use, long-lasting materials, and very stable.

Modular Construction

Individual layers can be assembled into a larger form.

Layered Depth

Stacked geometry builds visual depth from simple pieces.

Actual Fractal

The form references recursive triangular growth based on the Sierpinski gasket.
